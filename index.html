✨ What is the Summer Movement Challenge?

The Summer Movement Challenge is a fun, wellness initiative running from June 1 to September 30, 2026. All movement counts — not just walking! Cycling, swimming, yoga, kayaking, hiking, and fitness classes all convert to steps so everyone can join in no matter how they like to move.

You can join one, two, or all three challenges — as an individual, part of a team, or both at once.

🌈 3 ways to participate

1

Team distance

Form a team of up to 5 and combine your steps. Top 2 teams win a prize.

2

Solo distance

Go it alone and rack up as many steps as you can. Top 3 individuals win.

3

10K-a-day streak

Hit 10,000 steps every day. The person with the longest unbroken streak wins.

How to get started

1
Register below — enter your name, optional team name, and pick your challenges.
2
Track your movement each day and convert any activity to steps using the converter or the reference chart.
3
Submit your results every two weeks. Remember to convert activities before submitting!

FAQ

● Do all activities count?
Yes — all movement counts! Cycling, swimming, yoga, kayaking, and fitness classes all convert to steps.
● Can I join more than one challenge?
Absolutely — you can join as an individual, a team member, or both, and you can sign up for all three challenges.
● How do teams work?
Teams can include up to 5 . Pick a fun team name and work together to accumulate steps. The top 2 teams win a prize.
● How does the streak challenge work?
Hit 10,000 steps every day without missing one. The person with the longest unbroken streak at the end of the challenge wins.
● How do I track my progress?
Record your steps here and submit every two weeks. Remember to convert any activity to steps first.
